Focaccia with Caranelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries,and creamy brie.It Will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all- purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
